I originally wanted to make a blinky board, and accidentally clikced on the Pathfinder tutorial. After I realized that I am indeed
not making a Blinky Board, but something else, I was already an hour in and didn't feel like changing and restarting. So I 
continued and took on the challenge. 

For the schematics, I downloaded that parts the tutorial said I should, however, naming wasn't matching up. After asking around 
in slack, I found out that as long as the numbers are similar and I did download it from the right spot, I'm fine.
